LLM Book Club — Setup Steps
============================

1. Open PowerShell as Administrator and run:
   powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File "B:\ai\llm-book\setup\01_install_docker.ps1"

2. Restart your computer.

3. After restart, open PowerShell and run:
   powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File "B:\ai\llm-book\setup\02_start_app.ps1"

4. Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser.

5. Drag a book from D:\books into the upload area.

That's it. Come back to Claude Code and say "continue" to pick up where we left off.
